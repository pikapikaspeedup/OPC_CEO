# Priority Matrix: UX Improvements

| Improvement | Impact | Effort | Priority |
|:---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| **1. Soft-Refresh Transitions** | High | Low | **P0** (Critical) |
| **2. Custom Department Modal** | Medium | Medium | **P1** (Standard) |
| **3. Bento-Grid Refactoring** | High | High | **P2** (Structural) |

### Rationale
- **Soft-Refresh:** Polling is central to the product. Fixing the "jumpy" UI immediately improves professionalism with minimal refactoring (CSS transitions / Framer Motion).
- **Custom Modal:** Standardizes the brand experience for the primary growth action.
- **Bento-Grid:** Requires a structural layout change but aligns with the "Executive Summary" goal of a dashboard.
